About Patio Express

Patio Express is environmentally friendly and manufactured from HDPE milk jugs and water bottles recycled from landfills every year. We only use purified recycled plastic that is made with color pigments and Ultra-Violet (UV) stabilizers which result in colorful, maintenance free, and eco-friendly products.
